GalacticResource is the Corgan Studio Milky Way intelligence platform. Procedural spiral-arm background, Yale Bright Star Catalog (9,096 stars), 4,563 NASA Exoplanet Archive host systems plotted in true galactic coordinates, Sgr A* supermassive black hole at galactic center, Sol positioned 26,000 light-years from core, labeled spiral arms (Perseus, Sagittarius, Scutum-Centaurus, Norma, Outer, Orion Spur), notable nebulae (Orion, Eagle, Crab, Lagoon, Tarantula), globular clusters (Omega Centauri, 47 Tucanae, M13), Local Group galaxies (M31 Andromeda, M33 Triangulum, LMC, SMC). Part of the Resource Systems multi-planetary suite alongside TerraResource, MoonResource, MarsResource, SolarSystemResource, built by Corgan Studio.
